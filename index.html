    You've used ChatGPT to plan trips before. It gives you a great list, but then you have to copy-paste hotel names into Google Maps, open 10 tabs to check prices, and struggle to visualize the distance between the museum and the restaurant.

    TripCanvas changes that. We turn the AI's suggestions into a native, interactive app that lives right in your chat window.

    Key Features

    Compare Hotels Instantly

    Stop guessing what a 4-star hotel in Shinjuku looks like. We show a swipeable carousel of top-rated hotels with photos, prices, and direct booking links.

    Interactive Itinerary Maps

    Understand your trip at a glance. We plot daily activities on a map so you can see exactly where you are going and avoid backtracking.

    Dynamic Timelines

    How It Works: 3 Simple Steps

    Step 1: Connect

    Enable the TripCanvas extension in your ChatGPT settings. It takes 5 seconds.

    Step 2: Chat Naturally

    Ask ChatGPT as you normally would: "Plan a 3-day romantic getaway to Paris for under $500."

    Step 3: Interact

    Instead of a wall of text, a widget appears. Tap hotels, swipe through days, and explore the map.
